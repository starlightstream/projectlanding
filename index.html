Random Facts!

Did you know that Pomeranians have two coats? Their long outer coat repells moisture and dirt while their thick inner coat locks in warmth!

Pomeranians are part of the Spitz family.

Queen Victoria popularized the breed and helped bred them down to smaller sizes.

Pomeranians descended from German Spitz which weigh around 30-50lbs. Nowadays pomeranian standard weight is 3-7lbs!
