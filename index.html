Random Facts!

Did you know that Pomeranians have two coats? Their outer coat repells moisture and dirt while their inner coat locks in warmth!
Pomeranians are part of the Spitz family. There are 68 breeds in the spitz family and most orginate from the Artic region or Siberia.
Did you know that Queen Victoria popularized the smaller size of the breed? During her lifetime she managed to decrease the breeds size by half.
Pomeranians descended from German Spitz which weigh around 30-50lbs. Nowadays, the standard weight of pomeranians is 3-7lbs!
